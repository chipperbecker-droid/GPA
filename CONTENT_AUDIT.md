# GPA Content Preservation Map

Audit completed against the public legacy site on June 24, 2026. The live site was read with certificate verification disabled because the domain's current TLS certificate does not match the hostname. Public page copy, linked PDFs, page structure, and previously published attribution were inspected before the long-form preservation pass.

## Preservation standard

- Preserve specific explanations, qualifications, examples, and industry distinctions.
- Modernize grammar, hierarchy, and paragraph length without flattening Gary Penrod's experience-based voice.
- Keep factual claims that were already public, but do not turn old transaction or opportunity information into a current claim.
- Keep original PDFs available as primary archive resources.
- Document material that is omitted because it is empty, obsolete, duplicative, or potentially misleading.
