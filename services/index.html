Buy-side advisory

Industry-specific perspective

The same numbers can tell different stories in different businesses.

Building services companies are shaped by recurring contracts, labor, customer retention, service mix, geography, supervision, and owner dependence.

GPA’s work brings those realities into the conversation before decisions harden. The firm helps clients ask better questions, recognize issues earlier, and avoid forcing a building services company into a generic transaction formula.
