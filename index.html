Why specialization matters

A transaction is only as sound as the judgment behind it.

Generic M&A playbooks can miss the operating details that shape value and buyer confidence in the building services industry.

GPA brings the perspective of former operators, financial leaders, and advisors who understand the business beneath the numbers. That experience helps clients ask better questions, anticipate friction, and make decisions they can still respect in retrospect.

Industry context at every stage

  • Building service contractor operating dynamics
  • Labor model, contract mix, customer concentration, and market reach
  • Strategic, financial, and first-time buyer considerations
  • Transition planning for owners, teams, customers, and workforce

Gary A. Penrod

Operator experience. Industry leadership. Long-term perspective.

Before advising owners, Gary Penrod founded and operated a building services company, then sold it to a firm in the UK and assisted with additional acquisitions. He later formed Gary Penrod and Associates, Inc. (GPA).

Gary is a former president and board member of Building Service Contractors Association International (BSCAI), as well as a published industry author and recognized speaker.
