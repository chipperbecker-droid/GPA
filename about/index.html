About & credentials

Industry knowledge earned from inside the business.

Gary Penrod’s advisory work is grounded in his experience as a building service contractor, business seller, acquisition participant, industry leader, and long-term M&A advisor.

GPAFocused on the building services contractor industry since 1988

Gary A. Penrod

Founder & Managing Associate

Gary founded and operated a successful building services company for approximately fourteen years before selling the business to an international service group.

Following the sale, he assisted that organization with additional acquisitions. The experience gave him perspective on both sides of a transaction: the owner’s personal and operational concerns, and the acquirer’s need for sound information, disciplined evaluation, and a workable transition.

In 1988, Gary founded Gary Penrod & Associates, Inc. as an industry-specific consulting firm. GPA evolved to specialize in M&A intermediary and business advisory services for firms engaged in building services.
