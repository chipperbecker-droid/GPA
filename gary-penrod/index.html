Gary Penrod

CEO and Managing Associate

GPA’s CEO and Managing Associate continues to build on solid foundations, beginning with his founding of a building services company.

After operating that firm for 15 years, it was sold to a firm in the UK. Later, he formed Gary Penrod and Associates, Inc (GPA). He is a recognized speaker within the industry and has authored many articles for Services and other trade publications.

Active in Building Services Contractor’s Association, International (BSCAI), he served on its Board for many years and was also its President. Penrod is known for his general business accuracy and his knowledge of the building services industry and the people within its ranks. He is considered by many to be the authority in the merger and acquisition sector of the industry.
